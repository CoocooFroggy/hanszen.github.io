Standing Committees

Hanszen's Standing Committees are designated by our Constitution as essential to the functioning of the college, and thus are automatically renewed each year. It is because of the hard work of Standing Committees that we have things like Beer Bike, Associates, Hanszen sports teams, and awesome swag. If you want to get involved or have any questions/ideas for events, feel free to find or email the committee heads below!

Alumni

This committee is in charge of engaging with Hanszen alumni, especially during events like Willy Week and Homecoming. They also help connect Hanszenites to alumni for professional connections.

Associates

This committee takes care of Hanszen's beloved Associates! They are in charge of finding new Associates, planning semesterly Associates Nights, and making sure that Hanszenites are taking advantage of this great resource. To contact the Associates Committee, send an email to hanszenassociatechairs@gmail.com.

Awards

TThis committee is in charge of planning the Spring Semester Hanszen Awards Ceremony, which honors Hanszenites for their service to the college and handles Hanszen's prestigious travel scholarships.

Beer Bike

This committee plans and executes the best week of the year -- Willy Week! The Beer Bike Committee Heads (i.e. Coordinators), with the help of their committee, work for months to bring Hanszen the best possible Willy Week and Beer Bike, collaborate with the Bike and Chug Captains to put together a team for race day, and closely deliberate with Hanszen's Guardian regarding jacks on Willy Week.

College Night & Crane Day (CNCD)

This committee organizes Hanszen's two semesterly themed college nights and Crane Day in the spring. These events bring the entire Hanszen Family together, often including beverages, inflatables, and a guaranteed good time.

Merchandise

This committee designs, orders, subsidizes, and sells Hanszen merchandise. They are the reason Hasnzenites have the best swag on campus, and have designed items like tank tops, sweatpants, boxers, scarves, and even pens for all to enjoy.

Off-Campus (OC)

This committee is in charge of events that specifically relate to Hanszenites who live off-campus. They provide supplies and free weekly lunches, throw off-campus events, and make sure that off-campus Hanszenites are as engaged with the Hanszen community as anyone else.

Senior Events

This committee organizes events for Hanszen's graduating seniors, especially during Senior Week right before graduation. They help Hanszen send off its eldest members in style with events like wine and cheese nights, tubing, and Top Golf.

Service

This committee is Hanszen's philanthropy branch, that makes sure that we give back to our community. They organize several Outreach Days throughout the year and also put together Hanszen's male pageant, Mr. Hanszen.

Spaces/Permanent Improvements

This committee takes care of Hanzen's spaces, both doing maintenance as well as taking on large-scale renovation projects like the music room, TV Room, and Library.

Spirit

This committee makes sure Hanszenites are proud to be Hanszenites! They make sure Hanszen's events calendar is updated, decorate the commons with seasonal banners, and put together events to support our Hanszen athletes. Additionally, they put together great events like yearly ice skating at the Galleria Mall, pumpkin carving around Halloween-time, and a Cascarones celebration near Easter.

Sports

This committee organizes and supports Hanszen's college sports teams. They make sure we have teams for all of our sports, assign captains, manage equipment, and lead Hanszen to championships each year.

Kitchen

This committee manages the Hanszen College kitchen. They work with the spaces committee and EVP to ensure all Hanszenites have a clean place to cook!

Initiative Committees

Imagine getting Hanszen funding to share your hobbies, interests, and passions with the college-- this is what Initiative Committees are! This committee system, started in 2014, allows Hannszenites to get involved doing the things they love most. Initiative committees have included things like arts and crafts, hacking, and brewing. Check out this year's Initiative Committees below, and feel free to get in touch if you want to get involved. Otherwise, start thinking of your own ideas for an Initiative Committee (or check out some past Initiatives to bring back) and watch for applications in the spring!

Past Initiative Committees

If you are looking for inspiration for an Initiative Committee, look to the past! Below is a list of Hanszen's now-immortalized past Initiative Committees. If you want to bring one of them back, applications are in the spring!

Crafts

This committee provided fun crafts that would help relieve stress as well as occasionally provide small decorations for Hanszen. In addition to this, this committee had events making practical crafts, such as storage containers.

Theater

This committee took charge in producing yearly theatre shows and musicals -- a long-standing tradition at Hanszen. This committee had put on musicals, large-scale productions, revues, and even talent shows for the entertainment of Hanszen and the wider Rice comunity.

Hanszen Beyond the Hedges (HBTH)

This committee organized fun outings for Hanszenites to go out and explore the city of Houston. HBTH organized and sometimes subsidized trips to zoos, museums, shows and operas so that Hanszenites could take advantage of the wonderful city in which they live.
