Standing Committees

Hanszen's Standing Committees are designated by our Constitution as essential to the functioning of the college, and thus are automatically renewed each year. It is because of the hard work of Standing Committees that we have things like Beer Bike, Associates, Hanszen sports teams, and awesome swag. If you want to get involved or have any questions/ideas for events, feel free to find or email the committee heads below!

Initiative Committees

Imagine getting Hanszen funding to share your hobbies, interests, and passions with the college-- this is what Initiative Committees are! This committee system, started in 2014, allows Hanszenites to get involved doing the things they love most. Initiative committees have included things like arts and crafts, hacking, and brewing. Check out this year's Initiative Committees below, and feel free to get in touch if you want to get involved. Otherwise, start thinking of your own ideas for an Initiative Committee (or check out some past Initiatives to bring back) and watch for applications in the spring!

Past Initiative Committees

If you are looking for inspiration for an Initiative Committee, look to the past! Below is a list of Hanszen's now-immortalized past Initiative Committees. If you want to bring one of them back, applications are in the spring!

Crafts

This committee provided fun crafts that would help relieve stress as well as occasionally provide small decorations for Hanszen. In addition to this, this committee had events making practical crafts, such as storage containers.

Hanszen Beyond the Hedges (HBTH)

This committee organized fun outings for Hanszenites to go out and explore the city of Houston. HBTH organized and sometimes subsidized trips to zoos, museums, shows and operas so that Hanszenites could take advantage of the wonderful city in which they live.
