The Hanszen College Constitution outlines rules and goals for the governance of our college. You can view it below or download it here.

The Hanszen College Bylaws outline procedural rules for running the college, room draw, parking draw, and more. You can view it below or download it here.

The Hanszen College Judicial Code outlines basic guidelines for how the College Court operates. You can view it below or download it here.

The Hanszen College Election code outlines how we elect students for positions in Hanszen goverment. You can view it below or download it here.

Bills and Resolutions are a way for the college to implent procedures for a time span of one (1) academic year.

A passed bill is considered in effect until the end of the first cabinet of the following academic year. You can view a list of current bills below or download it here.

Any resolution in effect is listed after the bills. A resolution is considered in effect until the end of the first cabinet of the following academic year.
