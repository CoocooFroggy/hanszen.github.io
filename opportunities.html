Current Opportunities at Hanszen

New Student Representative (Cabinet) New Student Representative (SA) New Student Justice Building Justice Constitutional Review Committee

New Student Representative (Cabinet)

Type: Elected by all new students


Open to: New Students (including transfer students)


Number of positions: 3


Description: As a New Student Represntative to Hanszen Cabinet, you will represent you class in our student government. Your responsibilities include voting in all cabinet decisions, making sure new students' voices are heard, organizing events for your class, and supplying food for cabinet once a month.

New Student Representative (Student Association)

Type: Appointed by Dani (Hanszen President) and Alison (Hanszen Senator)


Open to: New Students (including transfer students)


Number of positions: 2


Description: As a New Student Represntative to the Student Association (Rice's campus-wide student government separate from all the college governments) you will represent both you class and Hanszen as a whole. Your responsibilities include making sure new students' and Hanszenites' voices are heard, as well as participating in various committees.

New Student Justice

Type: Elected by all new students


Open to: New Students (including transfer students)


Number of positions: 1


Description: As a New Student Justice, you will be a member of the Justice League as well as serving as a point of contact for your class. You will work with Alan (Hanszen CJ) and the other Associate Justices to form our college court, connect Hanszenites to resources, and peacefully resolve disputes.

Building Justice

Type: Appointed by Alan (Hanszen CJ)


Open to: New Students (including transfer students)


Number of positions: 1


Description: As a Building Justice, you will be a member of the Justice League as well as serving as a point of contact for your region (dorm building and especially your floor). You will work with Alan (Hanszen CJ) and the other Associate Justices to form our college court, connect Hanszenites to resources, and peacefully resolve disputes.

Constitutional Review Committee

Type: Appointed by Hemish and Adi (Hanszen Parliamentarians)


Open to: All Hanszenites


Number of positions: 4 (1 from each class)


Description: The Constitutional Review Committee is a group of Hanszenites, led by the Parliamentarians, that is reviewing our Constitution, Judicial Code, Election Code, and Bylaws.
