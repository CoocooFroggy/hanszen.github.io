About Hanszen Resources

Your peers and faculty want you to succeed at Rice and beyond. We have teams of people at Hanszen dedicated to helping you achieve your academic goals, caring for your well-being, connecting you to campus-wide resources, and creating a safer, more equitable community. Feel free to reach out, we are here for you!
