Additional O-Week Personnel

But it's not just O-Week advisors that are here during O-Week. There are even more Hanszenites that are here to welcome you to Rice and to Hanszen.

Adult Team (H-Team) O-Week Coordinators Peer Academic Advisors Photo/Video Team President Chief Justice Student Maintenance Representative

The H-Team is what Hanszen calls the dedicated team of adults who live at Hanszen and who provide endless wisdom and resources to all Hanszenites. They include the Magisters, whose house is by Old Section and the grove, three sets of Resident Associates (RAs) spread across Old Section and New Section, and a College Coordinator with her office in the Commons. Their job is to make sure Hanszen is running properly, throw excellent study breaks, and serve as mentors and confidants for Hanszenites to support us through the highs and lows of college life. Click here for more information.

Team inStAgraM is our hard-working and dynamic team of O-Week coordinators. Sarah, Arvind, and Marc have worked tirelessly since January to plan this week and make sure it is the best welcome we can give you to Rice and to Hanszen. See their Welcome Letter for more information.

Next we have Team Picturizing, Hanszen's O-Week Peer Academic Advisors. This team is here to help you with any and all of your academic needs during O-Week, primarily your course selection for Fall 2022. THey are also a great resource for advice on majors (though you don't need to declare a major until the spring of sophomore year), minors, and certificate programs. If you have a question, don't be afraid to stop them and ask them for advice. It's why they are here! There are also faculty advisors that you will have time to meet with during the week.

Doing the extremely important work of capturing all the wonderful experiences of O-Week is our Photo/Video team. You will often see them around Hanszen with their cameras, and at the end of O-Week we all gather to watch their movie and relive the past week.

Dani is our wonderful president here at Hanszen, and she is super excited to welcome you all! During O-Week you will find her helping the coordinators out, floating around the different O-Week groups, and introducing you to life at Hanszen. During the year, she leads Hanszen Cabinet, our very own student government that plays a huge role in life at Hanszen.

Welcome, new Hanszenites! My name is Alan and I serve as Hanszen's Chief Justice. Officially, my job is to enforce the college judicial code, lead Hanszen's Justice League, and serve as an intermediary between Hanszenites and other college/campus-wide officials. Although my job description includes enforcing rules, I am not here to police you. The Chief Justice position exists to help everyone understand the expectations of both our college and the university and to keep all students safe. I will discuss with all of you what the rules look like and how to protect yourself. I am also a resource if you feel at all unsafe, need someone to quiet down, or just need someone to vent to. My goal is to make sure you are comfortable at Hanszen.

I also happen to be one of Hanszen's website managers, and I have put together the O-Week section of the website just for you. I'm always looking for input and feedback from everyone, so please let me know if you have any questions or recommendations!

Light bulb went out? Broken A/C? Bed too high or too low for your liking? If you are experiencing any of these problems, then Kian, our Student Maintenance Representative (SMR), is your solution. He is trained in fixing common dorm problems, and is Hanszen's liaison to Housing and Dining (H&D). He also will periodically give updates from H&D.
